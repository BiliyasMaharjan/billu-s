Liverpool:

Liverpool competes in the Premier League, the top tier of English football. Founded in 1892, the club joined the Football League the following year and has played its home games at Anfield since its formation. Liverpool is one of the best supported clubs in the world. The club states that its worldwide fan base includes more than 200 officially recognised Supporters Clubs in at least 50 countries. The song "You'll Never Walk Alone", originally from the Rodgers and Hammerstein musical Carousel and later recorded by Liverpool musicians Gerry and the Pacemakers, is the club's anthem and has been sung by the Anfield crowd since the early 1960s.

Since the arrival of Jurgen Klopp back in 2015, he has transformed Liverpool to which was a sleeping giant to one of the best team in the Europe and winning UEFA Champions League, UEFA Super Cup, Premiere League title, FIFA club worldcup, FA cup, etc.

Formation:

Liverpool mostly use 4-3-3 formation with two full-backs, two center-backs, one defending midfielders, two center midfielders, and three up front. When Jurgen Klopp arrived Liverpool back in 2015, he experimented on different formation from 4-2-3-1 to 4-4-2. After rotating the whole team and experimenting on different playing style, he implemented gegenpressing in Liverpool's team.

And now, he is considered as one of the best coach in the world.
